About the Vibe

Open sky.
Slow evenings.
Loud memories.

Chill Vibes is an outdoor bar built for gatherings — birthdays under fairy lights, corporate mixers with a skyline view, weddings that spill into the night. We craft over thirty signature mocktails, curate the sound, and dress the space so all you have to do is show up and stay a while.
